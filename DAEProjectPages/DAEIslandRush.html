Description

As a group project we made the couch co-op game Island Rush. Which we published on Itch.io. The core gameplay loop revolved around going around the Island trying to collect as much food as you can before the Gods' anger reached it's peak. To collect food you'll need or create a net between 2 players and then try to drag the food along with you. Ofcourse the game isn't that easy. There are several obstacles in the way from overgrown hungry crabs that will try to kill you to the God himself who will try to mess with you through his range of different abilities.

My contibutions to this project:

  • Camera functionality
  • Base weapon pickup functionality
  • Main/Pause menu functionality
  • Player UI
  • Respawning
  • Some of the God mechanics
  • Assisted with player movement
